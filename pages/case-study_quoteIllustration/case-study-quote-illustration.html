“ART” is made of vintage fabric and has soft

The connection of art with horsemanship is in

SOLUTION
The nal composition consists of the ground
and sky levels. I meant to pull the viewer with
the beginning of the quote, “Horsemanship
is the” for the quote to read correctly. I used

objects of the scene to immerse lettering in the
space, but the letters are still readable because,
through gestalt principle, viewers can draw
imaginary line in places it is hidden.

An example of traditional horse training
Currently, people practice the wrong method
of horsemanship. Horses are beaten and abused
with so many metal gadgets that bring pain to

emotions, and behavior. Not the horse.”
Lastly, I put “Not the HORSE” part oating

the good future of horsemanship.
